About Me

Hi! My name is Ikhwan and I love building things that live in the digital world. My web development interest sparked back when I was 13 during the Harlem Shake meme era. I stumbled upon a piece of Javascript code that would make the elements on the page of the site that I was in to shake wildly. Another reason for my interest in web development is because I loved to mess around with 'inspect element' in the browser. I remember feeling like I was a hacker just because I could change the type for the element 'input' from 'password' to 'text' so that it would reveal the password in the input field.

As a web developer, I am fully aware that there is no end to learning technologies and things related to web development. Therefore, I am continuously in the process of learning new stuff on the internet.

  • Python and Django
  • PHP and Laravel
  • Flutter
  • Wordpress
  • Outsystems

Project

  • I developed a Django-based system that uses face recognition artificial intelligence to help lecturers of Fakulti Sains Komputer & Teknologi Maklumat (FSKTM) to record the attendance of their students. The system automatically scans the faces of students as they enter the classroom and records their attendance. The system also allows lecturers to view the attendance records of their students at any time.
